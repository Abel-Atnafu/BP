Personalized BP Diet Plan

Ethiopian–Fusion
Blood Pressure Plan

A strict 3-meal daily plan using foods you love, avoiding oats & salmon

👩 Female, 43 yrs ⚖️ 73 kg 🩺 BP 150/100
⚡ Follow strictly for 2–4 weeks to see measurable BP reduction
🥤

Keep Your Daily Juice!

Your beetroot + ginger + carrot juice is excellent — drink it every morning or afternoon. Beetroot nitrates are clinically shown to lower systolic BP by 4–10 mmHg. Don't stop this.

🌅 Breakfast
🍳
Morning Meal · ~380 cal

Firfir with Injera & Eggs

Ethiopian Enqulal Tibs (Spiced Eggs)
2 eggs scrambled with onion, tomato, green pepper, turmeric & black pepper — cooked in a tiny drop of olive oil. Served with a small piece of injera (teff is high in iron & magnesium).
Add-on Avocado Slices
½ ripe avocado sliced on the side — rich in potassium which directly counters sodium's BP-raising effect.
🍵 Drink
1 cup unsweetened hibiscus tea (Karkade) — studies show it lowers systolic BP by up to 7 mmHg. Or green tea.
💚 Teff in injera is high in magnesium — a key mineral for relaxing blood vessel walls
☀️ Lunch
🫘
Midday Meal · ~500 cal

Misir Wot & Vegetable Salad

Ethiopian Misir Wot (Red Lentil Stew)
1 cup red lentils cooked with onion, garlic, berbere (low salt), tomato & olive oil. Served with small injera or whole-grain bread. Lentils are loaded with potassium, folate & fiber — all BP-lowering.
International Mediterranean Salad
Tomato, cucumber, red onion, parsley, lemon juice & 1 tsp olive oil dressing. Zero salt — the lemon provides brightness. Add a handful of spinach or arugula.
💧 Drink
2 large glasses of plain water. You can add cucumber or lemon slices.
💚 Lentils provide ~730mg potassium per cup — potassium is the #1 mineral to counter high BP
🌙 Dinner
🍗
Evening Meal · ~430 cal

Ye'enqulal Tibs & Gomen

Ethiopian Ye'enqulal Tibs (Spiced Egg Stir-fry)
3 eggs scrambled or pan-fried with onion, garlic, tomato, green pepper, turmeric, black pepper & cumin. Cooked in 1 tsp olive oil only — no salt. Use lemon juice for brightness.
Ethiopian Gomen (Collard Greens)
Collard greens cooked with garlic, onion & ginger. Collard greens are extremely high in calcium and potassium — both critical for BP control.
International Sweet Potato (small)
½ boiled or baked sweet potato — excellent potassium source, and keeps you full. No butter or salt.
💚 Eggs are rich in potassium & magnesium — both directly lower BP. Gomen adds calcium for arterial health

📅 When Will You See Change?
Days 3–5
First signs: Less bloating, reduced headaches, slightly better energy as sodium clears your system
Week 1–2
Noticeable change: BP may drop 5–10 mmHg. You'll feel lighter, headaches should ease significantly
Week 3–4
Measurable results: BP reduction of 10–15 mmHg is realistic with strict adherence. Sustained improvement if you continue

This plan supports — but does not replace — medical treatment. At 150/100 with headaches, please continue seeing your doctor. If you feel dizzy, have chest pain, or your headache worsens, seek emergency care immediately.
